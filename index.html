Roark Automations

Roark Automations - AI Automation Platform

Roark had a strong Voice AI product but no clear idea of who they should target. Their message focused on features instead of trust, which made it hard for investors and customers to see the value. I reviewed their data, studied user behavior, and found their true high-value segment: mid-market Voice AI teams under pressure to scale with reliability.

I built an Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) around this insight and rebuilt their narrative around one principle: reliability is reputation. After the rollout, lead quality increased by 55%, engagement doubled among technical buyers, and investors gained confidence because the company finally had a focused story everyone understood.

Kestral AI

Kestral AI

Kestral built a brilliant AI product for product teams, but their story felt flat. They spoke in features instead of impact, which made the brand feel functional instead of valuable. They had traction but no momentum, and they were getting lost in a noisy market. I stepped in to reposition the company and build a go-to-market strategy that matched the strength of their product. Through market mapping and user insight sessions, I identified their real category as Intelligent Product Development, not “another AI tool.”

I shaped their brand around clarity, confidence, and emotional relief for overwhelmed product teams. I rebuilt their narrative, reshaped their messaging, and aligned their outbound, inbound, and partnership motions around this new position. Within 90 days, qualified inbound leads grew by 40%, engagement tripled on LinkedIn, and demo-to-close time dropped by 28%. Kestral went from a feature-heavy AI platform to the trusted intelligence layer for modern product teams.

EffiGov GovTech AI

EffiGov - GovTech Automation

EffiGov had a strong mission but no unified brand to express it. Their product helped cities serve residents with accuracy and care, yet the message felt scattered and too focused on technology. I stepped in to build a full brand book that captured the heart of their work. I built a narrative rooted in empathy, reliability, and inclusivity, and aligned their messaging, voice, identity, and strategy around that story. The result was a brand leaders understood instantly and a system that cut onboarding time by 30% while strengthening how the company showed its value to cities and partners.
