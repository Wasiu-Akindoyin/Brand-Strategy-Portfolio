Who I Am, What I Do

Most tech founders build strong products and just write about features while their buyers want outcomes. That gap is where deals slow down, interest fades, and most startups fails.

I’m Wasiu Akindoyin, a Brand and Marketing Strategist for SaaS, Web3, and AI founders. I turn complex products into clear narratives that accelerates brand growth, convert users and attract capital.

A SaaS team came to me with a messaging problem. Trials weren't converting. We rebuilt their brand positioning around buyer outcomes instead of product features. Trial-to-paid conversions went up 45%.

A voice AI platform was losing customers they'd already won. Retention was the problem, not acquisition. We repositioned the product around the results users were getting, not the technology behind it. Retention improved by 50%. Referrals grew 2.3x. All within four months.
